DVDs
I noticed that Dancer is lamenting the region restrictions on DVD players. I've heard a rumour that the players in New Zealand do not come with region restrictions as the NZ government classifies such stupidities as a trade restriction, anti-competitive, or something else it believes is illegal. Finance::Quote v1.00 at last!
At last Finance::Quote 1.00 has been released and is available for public consumption. Along with the new release I've also updated the Finance::Quote website so that it's not quite so content-poor.

Scotty is right that it takes a fair bit of time to release a project. I spent the better part of an hour or two uploading changelogs and writing announcements.

I guess now it's time for me to chase down Jon about that Perl Journal Article.

Work

Work
After a small discussion on slashdot I realised that if I were to take up contracting and work 1-2 days a week on average, I could happily maintain my current lifestyle (although I won't really be saving any money). This seems incredibly attractive, as a 5-day weekend every week would give me lots of time to do more fulfilling things than writing code for someone else. I'll need to sit down and figure out all the pros and cons of this scenario.

House

Jul 7 , 2000
House
We made an offer on the house, and someone else made an offer for the full asking price, so they got it. I'm not too upset, as there are plenty of other nicer houses out there, and in a few months time I'll be in a much better position to buy. What I find interesting is that both Ian and Sofie, and Benni and Tara are looking at buying houses now. It's almost looking like a Monopoly game.

Banks
One of the banks who I was talking about home-loans with wants to give me a complimentary credit card with an outrageously large credit limit. I'll be handing it back to them.

Diplomacy
Junwei is winning, and after this game I am going to have to take lessons from him about how he does this. Italy (Phil) is eating out of Junwei's hand, and is probably going to spur Junwei on to victory. I think we're going to have a hard time convincing Phil that possibly losing is better than helping Junwei and definitely losing. Unfortunately being the second largest power, everything thinks I'm trying to nobble Junwei so that I can win. The truth is I want to nobble Junwei so that I don't lose. I'm much rather force Junwei to draw than have him win the game. I'm researching stalemate lines to see what I can do.

Work
Negotiating with Dan to try and get a nine day fortnight. Believe it or not, I actually want more of a life. There's been a lot of proceedural stuff flying around recently, and not all of it is stable yet. It should be good once it is, because as bug-boy I want quality more than most.

A legacy version of one of our products is now finally finished, the customers using it having upgraded to the latest and greatest. This makes me very happy.
