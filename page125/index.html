Finance::Quote

Finance::Quote
Just received a letter informing me of a PHP version of Finance::Quote's yahoo function that should be released in the next couple of days. This is great news, as I've had a few requests for a PHP interface to Finance::Quote. I've pointed the relevant people at each other and hopefully they'll come to very happy arrangements.

I'm also thinking of writing a short guide on "How to submit a patch for Finance::Quote", as it seems to be a semi-regular requestion, and I most certainly do want the patches.

I'm still going to try to release 0.18 today, although I'm not certain of when I'll get the time to do it.

Diplomacy
